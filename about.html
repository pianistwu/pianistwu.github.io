Our Mission Statement

connected in music provides a spectrum of music services in order to help our clients build meaningful social connections through music-making. Serving New York City and nearby, we help people of all ages have creative, fulfilling interactions with others whether they are singing together, trying an instrument or participating in a drum circle. We specialize in helping older adults with dementia feel less isolated as they participate in our music groups. As licensed and board certified music therapists with strong teaching and performance skills, we bring a large toolkit to address the needs of our family of clients.
